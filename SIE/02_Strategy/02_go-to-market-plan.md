## 0. Preamble

This document operationalizes the phased roadmap defined in `[[SIE/S-00]]`. It outlines the specific strategies, tactics, and KPIs for bringing the Strategic Intelligence Engine (SIE) and the Master Hub offering to market. The plan is designed to be capital-efficient, prioritizing learning and validation before scaling.

---

## 1. Phase 1: The R&D Lab (Horizon: Now - 12 Months)

This phase is focused on perfecting the methodology and technology with a select group of pilot clients, minimizing risk while maximizing learning.

### **1.1. Core Objective**
- To validate the core offering, refine the `Ignition Sequence`, and build powerful case studies that will fuel Phase 2. This is a research and development phase, not a growth phase.

### **1.2. Brand & Positioning**
- **Brand:** `AdamBernard.com`
- **Positioning:** A high-touch, experimental consulting engagement. We are not selling a finished product; we are inviting partners to co-create a next-generation strategic asset.
- **Messaging:** "Partner with me to build the central intelligence for your business. Let's apply a proven methodology and cutting-edge AI to solve your biggest strategic challenges."

### **1.3. Target Audience**
- **Primary:** My direct professional network, including trusted past clients and respected peers.
- **ICP Focus:** A small, curated group of "Valerie" (VP of Marketing) and "David" (Agency Founder) personas from `[[SIE/S-01]]` who are innovators and early adopters. The key trait is a willingness to collaborate in an R&D context.

### **1.4. Offerings**
- **`Master Hub Foundation (Pilot)`:** A white-glove service to build the client's V1 Master Hub. It will be offered at a pilot program rate in exchange for deep feedback and case study rights.
- **`Strategic Engine Governance (Beta)`:** A heavily managed subscription to test the Engine's core automation features.

### **1.5. Sales & Marketing Tactics**
- **Sales:** 100% direct outreach. No inbound marketing.
  - One-to-one conversations via LinkedIn and email with curated contacts.
  - Presenting the concept and early results in private mastermind groups.
- **Marketing:** Thought leadership.
  - Writing articles on `adambernard.com` about the *philosophy* of "central intelligence" and the pain points of scattered knowledge.
  - No paid advertising.

### **1.6. Success Metrics**
- **[ ] Secure 4-6 paid pilot clients.**
- **[ ] Generate at least 2 publishable, in-depth case studies with quantifiable results.**
- **[ ] Achieve a 100% successful ingestion and structuring of pilot client data via the `Ignition Sequence`.**
- **[ ] Develop two core Engine automation features (e.g., Semantic Linking, Content Brief Generation) to a reliable, production-ready state.**

---

## 2. Phase 2: Productization (Horizon: 12 - 24 Months)

This phase leverages the credibility, case studies, and refined technology from Phase 1 to launch a scalable, partner-focused product.

### **2.1. Core Objective**
- To scale the business by empowering agency partners to sell and implement the SIE and Master Hub for their clients, creating a new recurring revenue stream.

### **2.2. Brand & Positioning**
- **Brand:** `LynxDigital.com` (or a dedicated product sub-brand like `Lynx Intelligence`).
- **Positioning:** A battle-tested, productized system for agencies to deliver high-value strategic services. We are selling reliability, profitability, and a competitive advantage.
- **Messaging:** "The proven system for productizing your agency's expertise. Install a Master Hub for your clients, activate it with our Strategic Intelligence Engine, and create an indispensable, recurring revenue relationship."

### **2.3. Target Audience**
- **Primary:** The "David" (Agency Founder) persona becomes the primary target. We are selling a "business-in-a-box" for strategic governance.
- **Secondary:** Direct outreach to the "Valerie" (VP of Marketing) persona, using case studies to prove ROI.

### **2.4. Offerings**
- **`Master Hub Foundation (Partner License)`:** A standardized package that agencies are certified to sell.
- **`Strategic Engine Governance (SaaS)`:** A productized, multi-tenant SaaS subscription with a partner revenue share model. Includes a partner dashboard for managing multiple client hubs.

### **2.5. Sales & Marketing Tactics**
- **Sales:** A combination of direct and channel sales.
  - Build a dedicated partner program with certification and co-marketing materials.
  - Direct outreach to a targeted list of high-potential agencies.
- **Marketing:** Content and community marketing.
  - Launch a dedicated product section on the Lynx Digital website featuring case studies, demos, and partner testimonials.
  - Run webinars for agency founders on "How to Productize Your Strategy."
  - Begin targeted advertising on platforms like LinkedIn to reach the "David" ICP.
  - Attend and speak at relevant industry events.

### **2.6. Success Metrics**
- **[ ] Onboard and certify the first 5 agency partners.**
- **[ ] Partners successfully close their first 3 `Master Hub Foundation` projects.**
- **[ ] Achieve the first $10k in Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) from the Governance subscription.**
- **[ ] Launch a V1 partner portal for managing client licenses and accessing enablement materials.**
