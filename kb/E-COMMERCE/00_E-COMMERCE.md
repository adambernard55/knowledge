# AI for E-commerce Marketing

Strategic integration and orchestration of AI tools across the e-commerce buyer journey — from discovery and acquisition, through on-site engagement and conversion, to post-purchase retention, loyalty, and advocacy.

## Core Frameworks

- **SMART Goals:** Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound objectives for AI initiatives
- **STRIVE Framework:** Strategic Fit, Technical Efficacy, ROI, Integration, Vendor Viability, Ethics — for evaluating AI tool categories

## Key Capability Areas

1. **AI Strategy & Foundations** — Strategic tool evaluation, integrated AI ecosystems, data strategy as foundation
2. **Discovery & Acquisition** — Market intelligence, competitor analysis, audience segmentation, content/SEO optimization
3. **On-Site Personalization & Engagement** — Product recommendations, dynamic content, site search, conversational AI
4. **Conversion Optimization & Pricing** — CRO, cart abandonment recovery, checkout optimization, dynamic pricing, inventory management
5. **Post-Purchase & Retention** — Personalized communication, churn prediction, loyalty programs, CLV optimization, brand advocacy
6. **Implementation & Scaling** — AI roadmaps, ROI measurement, iterative refinement, ethical governance, future-proofing
