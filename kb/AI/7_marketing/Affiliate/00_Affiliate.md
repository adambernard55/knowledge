# AI for Affiliate Marketing

Strategic application of AI tools and techniques to scale affiliate marketing programs — from partner discovery and vetting, through performance analytics and automation, to influencer integration and emerging trends.

## Core Frameworks

- **STP Framework:** Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning applied to affiliate partner selection
- **Marketing Mix Integration:** Aligning AI-driven affiliate tactics with broader marketing strategy

## Key Capability Areas

1. **Foundations & Strategy** — AI tool evaluation, program selection frameworks, ROI optimization principles
2. **Partner Discovery** — AI-powered identification, authenticity verification, audience-brand alignment
3. **Performance Analytics** — KPI tracking, attribution models, ROI calculation, fraud detection
4. **Automation & Personalization** — Dynamic link optimization, content personalization, prompt engineering for affiliate workflows
5. **Influencer Integration** — Identifying influencers for affiliate programs, outreach, tracking influencer-driven performance
6. **Trends** — Emerging developments including vibe marketing and evolving platform strategies
