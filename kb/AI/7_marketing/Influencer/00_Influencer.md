# AI for Influencer Marketing

Strategic use of AI to transform influencer marketing — from data-driven discovery and vetting, through relationship management and campaign optimization, to legal compliance and ethical governance.

## Core Frameworks

- **Data-Rich Identification:** AI-powered platforms analyzing audience demographics, engagement authenticity, and brand alignment
- **IRM (Influencer Relationship Management):** Centralized, AI-enhanced communication and contract management

## Key Capability Areas

1. **Foundations** — Evolution of influencer marketing, AI's impact on the discipline, goal-setting and metrics
2. **Discovery & Vetting** — AI-powered influencer identification, authenticity verification, selection strategy
3. **Relationship Management** — AI-enhanced IRM platforms, outreach automation, contract management
4. **Campaign Optimization** — Performance prediction, outreach personalization, real-time campaign adjustments
5. **Legal & Ethics** — Disclosure requirements, data privacy, authenticity standards, regulatory compliance
