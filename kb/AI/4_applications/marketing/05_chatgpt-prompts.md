# Reference Guide: ChatGPT Prompts

## Overview

ChatGPT prompts are instructions or queries used to guide the AI's responses. For optimal results, prompts should be specific and provide relevant context to help the model generate the most useful output. This guide provides a collection of prompts for various marketing, content, and SEO tasks.

## ChatGPT Prompts for Digital Marketing

- **LinkedIn Post:** Act as an experienced LinkedIn content creator. Write a 150-word LinkedIn post based on my article about [topic]. The post should highlight [number] key insights and pose a thought-provoking question to encourage professional discussion. Use a tone that is professional yet approachable. Include a clear call to action. Provide examples of your desired style and the source article: [Paste article here].
- **X (Twitter) Post:** Act as a social media specialist. Condense the main message of this blog post [link] into an impactful 280-character post. Emphasize one notable data point or conclusion. Start with an attention-grabbing opening and end with an incentive to read more.
- **YouTube Video Ideas:** Act as a content strategist for [type] businesses. Create [number] compelling YouTube video ideas for [company] on [topic]. For each, include a catchy title, a brief description, and how it aligns with the brand's target audience. Vary the formats (e.g., tutorials, interviews). Provide information about the company’s audience, brand, and products.
- **Email Welcome Sequence:** Craft a three-email welcome sequence for new subscribers. The first email should introduce our brand, the second should highlight our most popular product/service, and the third should offer a discount code. Provide details on [brand, offerings, and discount].
- **Ad Copy:** Write persuasive Google Ads copy for a campaign targeting [keyword], focusing on our product [product name]. Use this information: [product or campaign details].
- **Article Summary for Email:** Summarize this article [link] in 3-5 bullet points. Create a short email to our customers highlighting these points, explaining their relevance, and including a call to action (CTA).
- **Customer Feedback Survey:** Design a five-question customer satisfaction survey for our recent launch of [product name]. Include a mix of multiple-choice and open-ended questions.
- **Special Offer Email:** Craft an email for [company] to customers who haven’t purchased in [number of months]. Include this offer: [offer details].
- **Calls to Action (CTAs):** Write [number] CTA messages about [material]. I want [number] combinations of one header and one subheading.
- **YouTube Ad Script:** Write a 60-second YouTube script for our [product or service name]. It should highlight key benefits and include a clear CTA. Use this company information: [insert information].
- **Newsletter Content:** Write my weekly newsletter, including [number] headings and [number] paragraphs about [content].
- **Case Study:** Turn the data below into a case study with the format: (1) Challenge, (2) Solution, (3) Results. [Insert data].
- **Landing Page Copy:** Create landing page copy for a limited-time discount on [product or service]. Use [number] header combinations with these product features: [product feature list].
- **Referral Program Ideas:** Propose a referral program to incentivize existing customers to refer our [product or service].
- **Tagline Generation:** Act as a senior copywriter. Suggest [number] compelling taglines of [length] words for our new [product] targeting [specific demographic]. Capture our unique value proposition: [insert product information].
- **Lead Magnet Design:** Design a lead magnet or downloadable resource to attract potential customers to our website [website URL]. Here is information about our product: [Insert product information].
- **Infographic Outline:** Create the structure for an infographic highlighting the key benefits of our [product or service]. Here is information to help: [Insert product information].
- **Instagram Story Ideas:** Come up with [number] Instagram Story ideas for [company name] relevant to [target audience] using a brand voice that is [brand voice].

## ChatGPT Prompts for Content Creation

- **Compelling Introduction:** Act as an experienced content writer in the [field] field. Write a 100-word introduction for a blog post on [topic] aimed at [type of audience]. Incorporate these keywords: [keywords]. The intro should follow Problem-Agitation-Solution (PAS) principles.
- **Video Outline:** Act as a video content strategist. Develop a detailed outline for a step-by-step tutorial on [topic]. The outline should include an engaging intro, defined steps, visual cue suggestions, and a strong CTA. Aim for an 8-10 minute video.
- **Content Calendar:** Act as a content strategist specializing in SEO. Craft a content calendar with [number] blog post ideas for the next quarter, incorporating these keywords: [keywords]. Mix content types (how-to guides, listicles) and present in this format: [Insert desired format].
- **Editing for Clarity:** Act as a professional editor. Rewrite the following paragraph to improve clarity, simplify complex sentences, and enhance readability while maintaining the original meaning: [Insert paragraph here].
- **Email Newsletter Series:** Act as an email marketing specialist. Write a series of five email newsletters on [topic], each around 300 words with a catchy subject line and a clear CTA for an audience of [describe target audience].
- **Checklist Creation:** Create a checklist for conducting market research in the [your industry] industry before launching a new product.
- **Discussion Questions:** For a blog post on [topic], generate [number] discussion questions to encourage reader engagement and comments.
- **Podcast Script:** Write a script for a podcast episode discussing the benefits of [topic].
- **Whitepaper Development:** Develop a whitepaper on [topic] under [number of words]. Use this information: [Insert additional information].
- **Paragraph Rewriting:** Rewrite this paragraph as a bulleted list: [Insert paragraph].
- **Interview Summary:** Summarize the main insights and actionable takeaways from this expert interview in [number] words or less: [Paste interview transcript].
- **Article Summaries for Social Media:** Summarize the three most important takeaways from this blog post on [topic] in a bulleted list suitable for sharing on LinkedIn: [Insert blog post copy].
- **Cold DM/Outreach:** Write a short DM to [creator/business] persuading them to partner with me for [topic]. Include 2-3 reasons why a partnership makes sense. Here is information on them: [Insert information].
- **Content Trimming:** Trim this content down to fewer than [number] [sentences/words/characters]: [Insert content].
- **Editing for Flow:** Pretend you’re an editor with [years] of experience in [your industry]. Edit my text for flow and conciseness: [Paste text].
- **Brand Voice Revision:** Rewrite this blog post excerpt to better reflect our brand voice, which is [describe brand voice]: [Insert excerpt].
- **HTML Jumplinks:** Write HTML hyperlinks for these H2 headers with header tags: [headers].
- **Subtopic Ideation:** I'm developing a content hub on [main topic]. Come up with 3-5 subtopic ideas that dive deeper into specific aspects of the main topic.

## ChatGPT Prompts for SEO

- **Meta Description:** Act as a professional content writer. Write a compelling meta description (max 150 characters) for my blog post on [topic]. It must include the primary keyword [keyword] and entice users to click. Here is the article's introduction: [Insert introduction].
- **FAQ Section:** Act as an SEO expert. For an article on [detailed article topic], generate five frequently asked questions that incorporate these keywords: [keywords]. Provide a clear, concise answer (under 150 words) for each.
- **Subheadings:** Act as an SEO content strategist. Write 10 compelling, SEO-friendly subheadings for a blog post titled [title]. The subheadings should be logical, engaging, and incorporate relevant keywords. Here is the article's outline: [Insert outline].
- **Schema Markup:** Act as a technical SEO expert. Create schema markup using JSON-LD for a page on [topic] that incorporates [relevant schema elements]. Ensure it adheres to schema.org standards. Use these details: [Insert specific page details].
- **Article Improvements:** Analyze the top three ranking articles for my keyword [keyword] and identify opportunities to optimize my content for better readability, comprehensiveness, and user engagement. Here is the text of the articles: [insert article text].
- **Blog Post Title Ideas:** Act as an SEO copywriter. Suggest [number] compelling blog post titles using the primary keyword [keyword], considering search intent and audience pain points.
- **Internal Linking:** Based on my sitemap, suggest 3-5 internal linking opportunities with anchor texts of no more than [number] words for each. Here is my sitemap: [Insert sitemap].
- **Robots.txt File:** Craft a robots.txt file for my website that allows crawlers access to important content while blocking unnecessary directories. Here is the list of subfolders to exclude: [Insert list].
- **Content Gap Analysis:** Using my sitemap URLs, identify potential content gaps where I could create new content to target relevant keywords. Here is my sitemap: [Insert sitemap].
- **Article Outlining:** Create an outline for an article about [topic]. The brand voice is [description]. Structure it with [first section], [second section], and [last section].
- **Hreflang Tags:** Generate hreflang tags for a webpage targeted to [country] in [language]. The page also has versions for: [Insert list of countries and languages].
- **XML Sitemap Creation:** Act as a technical SEO specialist. Create an XML sitemap including these URLs: [Insert list of URLs].
- **RegEx for GSC:** Generate a RegEx in RE2 syntax to [description of what to match/filter] for Google Search Console.
- **Long-tail Keywords:** List all the long-tail queries related to [keyword].
- **Clustered Keyword List:** Create a keyword list for my SEO agency around [topic]. Cluster the list based on the sales funnel (top, middle, bottom).
- **General Keyword List:** Generate a list of at least 20 keywords related to [topic].
