# Optimizing Content for AI Citation (GEO)

One of the most critical applications of AI in modern marketing is adapting content strategy to ensure it gets discovered, cited, and recommended by generative AI models like ChatGPT. This practice is known as **Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)**.

GEO combines data-driven on-page tactics with off-page authority-building signals to maximize visibility within AI-generated answers.

The complete, actionable framework for this strategy is maintained within the SEO Knowledge Base.
