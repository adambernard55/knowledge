# AI for Affiliate Marketing

Strategic application of AI tools and techniques to scale affiliate marketing programs — from strategic alignment and partner discovery, through performance analytics and automation, to creator integration and emerging trends.

## Core Frameworks

- **STP Framework:** Segmentation, Targeting, Positioning applied to affiliate partner selection and campaign design
- **Marketing Mix Integration:** Aligning AI-driven affiliate tactics with broader marketing strategy
- **STRIVE Evaluation:** Strategic Fit, Technical Efficacy, ROI, Integration, Vendor Viability, Ethical Compliance for tool selection

## Key Capability Areas

1. **Foundations & Strategy** — Strategic alignment of AI with marketing objectives, core AI concepts (ML, NLP, data analytics, automation), tool categories, SMART goal setting, data readiness
2. **Partner Discovery & Vetting** — AI-powered identification, algorithmic partner analysis, NLP sentiment analysis, authenticity verification, audience-brand alignment, STRIVE evaluation framework
3. **Content Optimization** — AI content ideation, PTCF prompt engineering framework, headline optimization, strategic link placement with NLP, brand voice consistency, LSI keyword integration
4. **Performance Analytics** — Strategic KPIs, multi-touch attribution models, ROI calculation, AI dashboards, fraud detection, compliance monitoring, predictive analytics
5. **Automation & Personalization** — Dynamic link optimization, content personalization, AI chatbots, automated communication triggers, customer journey mapping, competitive analysis, dynamic commissions, ethical governance
6. **Creator Integration** — AI-driven creator identification, deep audience analysis, brand safety vetting, personalized outreach, dark social tracking, influencer attribution, communication automation
7. **ROI Tools & Trends** — Platform-specific ROI optimization (Affluent, Trackonomics, Publisher Discovery), vibe marketing, hyper-personalization, cookieless tracking, ethical landscape evolution
