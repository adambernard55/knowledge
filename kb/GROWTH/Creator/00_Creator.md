# AI for Creator Marketing

Strategic use of AI to transform creator marketing — from data-driven discovery and vetting, through relationship management and campaign optimization, to legal compliance and ethical governance.

## Core Frameworks

- **Data-Rich Identification:** AI-powered platforms analyzing audience demographics, engagement authenticity, and brand alignment
- **IRM (Influencer Relationship Management):** Centralized, AI-enhanced communication and contract management

## Key Capability Areas

1. **Foundations** — Evolution from influencer to creator marketing, AI's impact on the discipline, goal-setting and metrics
2. **Discovery & Vetting** — AI-powered creator identification, authenticity verification, brand-creator matching
3. **Relationship Management** — AI-enhanced IRM platforms, outreach automation, contract management, long-term partnerships
4. **Campaign Optimization** — Performance prediction, content and strategy optimization, ROI tracking, A/B testing
5. **Legal & Ethics** — FTC disclosure requirements, data privacy, authenticity standards, regulatory compliance
