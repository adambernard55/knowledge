# AI for E-commerce Marketing

Strategic application of AI tools and frameworks to orchestrate the entire e-commerce buyer journey — from foundational strategy and customer acquisition, through on-site engagement and conversion optimization, to post-purchase retention and long-term scaling.

## Core Frameworks

- **SMART Goals:** Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound objectives applied to every AI initiative across the buyer journey
- **STRIVE Framework:** Strategic Fit, Technical Efficacy, ROI, Integration, Vendor Viability, Ethics — a structured evaluation methodology for selecting AI tool categories

## Key Capability Areas

1. **Strategy & Foundations** — Core AI concepts for e-commerce (NLP, computer vision, predictive analytics, ML), the STRIVE evaluation framework, and AI tech stack architecture
2. **Customer Acquisition** — AI-driven market and competitor intelligence, audience segmentation (RFM, behavioral clustering, predictive CLV), and content/SEO optimization
3. **On-Site Engagement** — Product recommendation engines, dynamic website personalization, optimized site search, and conversational AI chatbot strategy
4. **Conversion Optimization** — CRO and cart abandonment recovery, dynamic pricing and inventory management, checkout optimization and payment security
5. **Post-Purchase Retention** — Personalized post-purchase communication, churn prediction and loyalty program optimization, brand advocacy and community engagement
6. **Implementation & Scaling** — Overarching AI strategy and ethical governance, performance measurement and ROI, scaling initiatives, and future-proofing against emerging trends
7. **Capstone Planning** — Strategic AI action plan development methodology and strategy validation frameworks
