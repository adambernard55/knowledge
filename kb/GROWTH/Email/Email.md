# AI for Email & CRM Marketing

Strategic application of AI tools and techniques to transform email and CRM marketing — from foundational strategy and advanced personalization, through intelligent automation and influencer outreach, to campaign optimization and future-proofing.

## Core Frameworks

- **SMART Goals:** Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound objectives applied to AI adoption across email marketing initiatives
- **AI Email Marketing Stack:** Understanding how ML, NLP, and data analysis tools integrate within the email technology ecosystem

## Key Capability Areas

1. **Strategic Foundations** — AI's strategic role in modern email, core AI concepts (ML, NLP, data analysis), customer-centricity, email tool landscape evaluation, and SMART goal-setting for AI adoption
2. **Advanced Personalization** — Behavioral, contextual, and predictive personalization strategies, granular audience segmentation, dynamic content and recommendations, A/B testing, and balancing automation with the human touch
3. **Intelligent Automation** — AI-powered email automation strategy, advanced triggered emails and behavioral targeting, intelligent follow-up sequences, onboarding and re-engagement workflows, and hands-on workflow design
4. **Influencer Outreach** — Email as an influencer amplifier, AI-powered influencer identification and segmentation, personalized outreach and proposal generation, automated outreach sequences, and ROI measurement
5. **Campaign Optimization** — Subject line and content optimization, send-time optimization and deliverability, campaign analytics and reporting, future-proofing strategies, and legal/ethical compliance (GDPR, CCPA, CAN-SPAM)
