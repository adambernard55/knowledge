# AI for Social Media Marketing

Strategic integration of AI across social media marketing — from core strategy and organic growth, through paid media and analytics, to building AI-powered automation systems.

## Core Frameworks

- **STRIVE Framework:** Strategic Fit, Technical Efficacy, ROI, Integration, Vendor Viability, Ethics for AI tool evaluation
- **SMART Goals:** AI implementation planning with measurable objectives

## Key Capability Areas

1. **Core Strategy** — Strategic imperative for AI in social media, core technologies, ecosystem mapping, ethics and governance
2. **Organic Growth** — AI-driven engagement, community building, content optimization for organic reach
3. **Paid Media** — AI-powered advertising, audience targeting, bid optimization, creative testing
4. **Intelligence & Analytics** — Social listening, sentiment analysis, competitive intelligence, performance measurement
5. **AI Systems** — End-to-end AI marketing systems, social media agents, workflow automation
