# Synthesia

Synthesia is an AI-powered video generation platform that allows users to create professional, studio-quality videos without needing cameras, microphones, or actors. It uses advanced text-to-speech and avatar generation technology to produce videos featuring lifelike presenters who deliver custom scripts in multiple languages.

## **Key Features**

- **AI Avatars Library:** Access over 150 fully licensed avatars across diverse appearances and languages.  
- **Custom Avatars:** Enterprise users can create personalized digital avatars for brand consistency.  
- **Text-to-Speech Engine:** Supports 120+ languages and accents for global reach.  
- **Customizable Templates:** Pre-built templates for explainers, onboarding, and training videos.  
- **Video Editing Tools:** Add text overlays, images, shapes, and screen recordings directly in the browser.  
- **Integrations:** Compatible with PowerPoint, Synthesia API, Learning Management Systems (LMS), and stock media libraries.  
- **Consistent Branding:** Options to upload logos, fonts, and brand colors to maintain corporate identity.  

## **Use Cases**

- **Corporate Training:** Quickly generate training and onboarding videos at scale.  
- **Product Explainers:** Transform marketing scripts into polished video explainers in minutes.  
- **Localized Marketing:** Translate and localize videos for multiple regions or languages effortlessly.  
- **Internal Communications:** Share company updates through engaging video messages instead of email.  
- **Sales Enablement:** Create personalized pitch videos or video FAQs with on-brand avatars.  

## **Pricing Overview**

Synthesia operates on a **subscription-based model:**

- **Starter Plan:** Around $30/month — ideal for individuals creating shorter videos.  
- **Business & Enterprise Plans:** Offer higher minute limits, API access, team collaboration, and custom avatars.  
- **Pricing Basis:** Typically based on the number of **video minutes generated per month** and advanced feature access.  
- *Always check Synthesia’s website for the latest plan details, usage quotas, and enterprise options.*

## **Expert Notes & Tips**

- Synthesia is excellent for **scalable video production**—especially for training, tutorials, or multilingual marketing.  
- Lifelike avatars are professional and consistent, though they may appear slightly synthetic—adjust pacing and tone for realism.  
- **Scriptwriting is key:** shorter, conversational text performs best on-camera.  
- Use **captions and branding assets** for accessibility and corporate polish.  
- For creative storytelling or cinematic visuals, pair Synthesia videos with b-roll or post-editing tools like RunwayML or Descript.
