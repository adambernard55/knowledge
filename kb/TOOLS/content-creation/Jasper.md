# Jasper

**Jasper** (formerly Jarvis) is a leading AI content creation platform designed for marketing teams, agencies, and businesses. It uses advanced generative AI to accelerate the production of high-quality copy, blog posts, articles, emails, ads, and visuals—all while maintaining your brand voice.

## **Key AI-Powered Features**
- **Brand Voice & Knowledge Base:** Trains Jasper on your brand’s tone, product data, and writing style for fully on-brand content.
- **Long-Form Assistant (“Boss Mode”):** Ideal for writing complete blogs, reports, or sales pages with guided prompts and contextual memory.
- **Content Templates:** Over 50 pre-built frameworks for creating ads, emails, product descriptions, or headlines instantly.
- **Jasper Art:** A text-to-image generator for creating royalty-free visuals that align with your written content.
- **Tone Adjustment & Rephrasing:** Rewrite, simplify, or enhance sentences while keeping the intended message.
- **Browser Extension:** Bring Jasper’s AI writing capabilities to any webpage or CMS, including Google Docs, WordPress, and social platforms.
- **Team Collaboration:** Manage brand assets, workflows, and multiple workspaces for content teams.
- **Plagiarism Detection:** Built-in Copyscape integration ensures all generated content is original.

## **Marketing Use Cases**
- **Content Marketing:** Write SEO-optimized blog posts, articles, and eBooks to scale production without losing quality.
- **Performance Marketing:** Generate and test multiple ad copy variants across Google and Meta networks.
- **Social Media:** Create consistent captions, hooks, and posts for diverse audience segments.
- **Email & Sales Copy:** Draft newsletters, drip campaign content, and persuasive calls to action.
- **Brand Storytelling:** Use Jasper’s tone matching and long-form workflows to maintain your authentic brand voice.
- **E-commerce:** Generate product descriptions, category text, and promotional copy for online stores.

## **Pricing Overview**
Jasper operates on a **subscription** model with tiered plans based on usage and features:
- **Creator Plan:** For individuals creating personal or small-business content.
- **Pro Plan:** Adds team collaboration, multiple brand voices, and advanced templates.
- **Business Plan:** Enterprise-level features including brand governance, API access, and advanced admin controls.
- **Free Trial:** A limited-time trial is typically offered for first-time users.

*Pricing varies by plan; always check the latest details at [https://www.jasper.ai/pricing](https://www.jasper.ai/pricing).*

## **Expert Notes & Tips**
Jasper stands out for its **Brand Voice** and **Campaign Workflows**—making it one of the best AI tools for maintaining consistency across varied content types. It’s AI-smart but **human-guided**, excelling at first-draft generation and ideation.

**Tips:**
- Use **Brand Voice** and **Knowledge Base** to avoid generic or off-brand output.
- Combine **Jasper Art** with text templates for cohesive visual storytelling.
- Use **[[ChatGPT (OpenAI)]]** for exploratory ideation or data-driven brainstorming, and Jasper for brand-locked execution.
- Integrate Jasper outputs with workflow tools like **[[Make.com]]** for automated publishing or review pipelines.
