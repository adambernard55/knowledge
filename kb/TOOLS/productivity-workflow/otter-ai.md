# Otter.ai

## What It Is
**Otter.ai** is an AI-powered note-taking and transcription tool designed for meetings, interviews, lectures, and collaborative discussions. It listens to conversations in real-time, generates accurate transcriptions, and produces concise summaries â€” all while identifying speakers and capturing slides shared during virtual meetings.

Originally launched as a voice transcription app, Otter.ai has grown into a full-fledged meeting intelligence platform trusted by enterprise teams and individuals for productivity and knowledge capture.

## Key Features
- **Real-Time Transcription:** Transcribes speech into text instantly during meetings or recordings.
- **Speaker Identification:** Automatically detects and labels multiple voices for organized transcripts.
- **Smart Summaries:** Generates key points, action items, and highlights automatically after each meeting.
- **Meeting Integrations:** Works seamlessly with Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams.
- **Collaboration Tools:** Lets teams comment, tag, and share transcripts directly within the Otter workspace.
- **Audio Playback & Search:** Replay any part of a meeting and search by keywords, phrases, or speaker.
- **Mobile & Desktop Apps:** Syncs across devices and supports manual uploads or live recordings.

## Use Cases
- **Meeting Documentation:** Automatically capture and summarize important discussions with clear action items.
- **Content Creation:** Turn interviews, webinars, and podcasts into draft text for blogs or reports.
- **Academic Use:** Record lectures or interviews and annotate transcripts for study or research.
- **Team Collaboration:** Share searchable transcripts across teams for easy recall and accountability.
- **Accessibility:** Improve inclusivity by providing real-time captions and notes.

## Pricing Overview
**Otter.ai** offers a **Freemium** model:
- **Free Plan:** Limited monthly transcription minutes and basic features.
- **Pro ($16.99/month):** More recording hours, custom vocabulary, and summary capabilities.
- **Business ($30/month per user):** Team workspaces, advanced admin controls, and enterprise integrations.
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing for large organizations with compliance, SSO, and dedicated support.

*Pricing subject to change; confirm at [https://otter.ai/pricing](https://otter.ai/pricing).*

## Expert Notes & Tips
Otter.ai is widely used by professionals, educators, and journalists who need reliable meeting summaries without manual note-taking. Its ability to identify speakers, generate summaries, and integrate directly into online meeting platforms makes it one of the most accessible productivity tools for both individuals and teams.

For the best transcription accuracy:
- Use high-quality microphones and clear speech.
- Add proper names and industry terms to your Custom Vocabulary.
- Record directly through Otter when possible to reduce errors from imported recordings.
