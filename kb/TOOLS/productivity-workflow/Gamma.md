# Gamma

## What It Is
**Gamma** is an AI-powered storytelling tool that converts written content—such as notes, outlines, or reports—into beautiful, interactive presentations and documents. Instead of traditional slide software like PowerPoint or Google Slides, Gamma provides a fluid, web-first experience where content adapts dynamically to any device or context.

Built with simplicity and collaboration in mind, Gamma allows teams and individuals to create visually compelling materials in minutes without touching a design tool. It’s especially popular among startups, educators, and marketers who value clean, interactive storytelling.

## Key Features
- **AI-Powered Generation:** Turns raw text or bullet points into full decks or webpages instantly.
- **Web-First Format:** Outputs presentations that feel more like interactive websites than static slides.
- **Beautiful Templates:** Offers professionally designed templates and color palettes that adapt automatically to content.
- **Customization Controls:** Edit layouts, swap themes, or adjust visuals without breaking design consistency.
- **Media Embeds:** Natively integrates videos, charts, and GIFs for interactive storytelling.
- **Collaboration Tools:** Supports real-time co-editing, sharing, and comments for teams.
- **Analytics (Pro & Team):** Tracks viewer engagement and time spent per section.

## Use Cases
- **Business Presentations:** Quickly generate investor decks, startup pitches, or marketing proposals.
- **Content Marketing:** Turn blog posts or scripts into immersive visual summaries.
- **Education & Training:** Transform lesson plans into interactive learning materials that adapt well across devices.
- **Knowledge Sharing:** Create wiki-style documentation or reports that stay readable and branded.
- **Personal Portfolios & Resumes:** Use Gamma’s web-first layouts to build modern, minimal personal pages.

## Pricing Overview
Gamma uses a **Freemium** model:
- **Free Plan:** Basic AI creation and editable themes with limited exports.
- **Pro ($16/month):** Advanced customization, analytics, private sharing links, and unlimited decks.
- **Team ($30/user/month):** Shared brand assets, collaboration tools, and admin options.
- **Enterprise:** Custom plans offering white-labeling, SSO, and dedicated support.

## Expert Notes & Tips
Where **Beautiful.ai** focuses on design automation for slide decks, **Gamma** reimagines the entire presentation experience for the web. It’s ideal for fast content iteration and storytelling with AI assistance, offering both creativity and simplicity. Gamma’s “webpage-like” decks perform better for asynchronous communication—perfect for distributed or remote teams.

**Pro Tips:**
- Start from text or Markdown drafts—Gamma will handle layout and flow.
- Use the interactive “cards” format to reduce clutter and improve pacing.
- Combine Gamma with **Notion AI** or **ChatGPT** to prepare pre-formatted outlines before importing.
- Publish decks as shareable links for online access with built-in analytics.
