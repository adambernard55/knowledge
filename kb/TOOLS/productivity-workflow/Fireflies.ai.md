# Fireflies.ai

## What It Is
**Fireflies.ai** is an AI-powered meeting assistant that automatically records, transcribes, summarizes, and indexes voice conversations across video conferencing platforms. It serves as a smart companion for teams and professionals who want to focus on discussions — not note-taking.

Once invited to a meeting, Fireflies joins as a participant, captures the conversation in real time, and delivers searchable transcripts and summaries afterward. With deep integrations across communication and CRM tools, it helps sales, marketing, and operations teams store insights where they matter most.

## Key Features
- **Automatic Recording & Transcription:** Captures meetings across Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, and more.
- **AI Summaries:** Generates concise meeting recaps with key topics, tasks, and takeaways.
- **Voice Search:** Search and replay any part of a call using keywords or speaker filters.
- **CRM & Workflow Integrations:** Syncs transcripts and notes directly to tools like HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, and Notion.
- **Collaboration Tools:** Add comments, reactions, and highlights directly to transcript timelines.
- **Multi-Speaker Detection:** Identifies different voices and preserves conversational structure.
- **Meeting Insights:** Offers analytics dashboards showing talk time, sentiment, and engagement patterns.

## Use Cases
- **Team Meetings:** Automatically generate searchable notes and summaries after every internal call.
- **Sales & Customer Success:** Capture detailed client conversations and push structured notes directly into CRM records.
- **Recruiting:** Review interview transcripts and candidate notes more efficiently.
- **Operations:** Track decisions and follow-ups without manual minute-taking.
- **Podcasts & Research:** Create written records of interviews or brainstorming sessions for content repurposing.

## Pricing Overview
**Fireflies.ai** follows a **Freemium** pricing model:  
- **Free Plan:** Limited transcription minutes and core features.  
- **Pro ($10/user per month):** Unlimited transcription and AI summaries.  
- **Business ($19/user per month):** Collaboration tools, CRM integrations, and advanced analytics.  
- **Enterprise:** Customizable plans with compliance, security, and admin controls.  

*Exact pricing may vary — check [https://fireflies.ai/pricing](https://fireflies.ai/pricing).*

## Expert Notes & Tips
Fireflies.ai stands out for its **collaboration-first design** and strong **CRM integration ecosystem**. Its AI model accurately captures context and sentiment, making it particularly valuable for sales and customer success workflows. Teams that record multiple calls daily benefit most, especially when paired with automated forwarding to Slack or HubSpot.

To get the best results:
- Invite Fireflies to meetings directly or link your calendar for automatic capture.
- Label speakers regularly to improve transcription accuracy.
- Leverage the “Smart Search” feature to find action items or sentiments instantly.
